Release 0: Equip Your New Robot
- Determine which floor button to press:
  - Examine floor buttons
    - If destination floor < current floor
      - Subtract one floor until you reach destination
    - Else if destination floor > current floor
      - Add one floor until you reach destination
    - Else 
      - You are at your destination

Release 1: Find a Real-Life Algorithm
- Determine when to continue driving after a stop sign:
  - If there are people or cars nearby
    - Come to a complete stop
    - Patiently wait
    - Yield when necessary until your turn
  - If there are no people or cars nearby
   - Come to a complete stop
   - Look around and wait three seconds
   - Continue driving
